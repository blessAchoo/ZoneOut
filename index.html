How it Works

This website allows the user the opportunity to take a step back from the issues and hardships of life by creating an environment tailored to your situation. Take a moment to learn how the website allows you to zone out.

1. Spend some time journaling about your day. This experience is intended to be therapeutic, so try to write about some of the hardships you faced and how they made you feel. Don't worry, our site doesn't store your data!

2. Click the "Generate" button and allow the site a couple moments to generate your environment. To ensure the best experience, please use headphones.
